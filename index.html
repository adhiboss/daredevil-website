The Origin Story

As a young boy, Matt Murdock was blinded in a tragic accident involving radioactive chemicals. While he lost his sight, his remaining senses were heightened beyond human limits.

Raised by a boxer who believed in honor and sacrifice, Matt learned that true strength comes from endurance. After his father was murdered by the criminal underworld, Matt made a choice — to fight back.

Armed with discipline, faith, and pain, he became Daredevil — a symbol criminals fear and the innocent depend on.
